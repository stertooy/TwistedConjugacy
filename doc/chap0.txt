  [1XAbstract[101X
  [33X[0;0YThe  [12XTwistedConjugacy[112X  package  provides  methods  to calculate Reidemeister
  classes,  numbers,  spectra  and  zeta  functions,  as well as other methods
  related  to  homomorphisms, endomorphisms and automorphisms of groups. These
  methods  are,  for the most part, designed to be used with finite groups and
  polycyclically presented groups.[133X
